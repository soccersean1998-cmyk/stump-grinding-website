<section id="faq">
<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>What is stump grinding?</h3>
<p>Stump grinding uses a specialized machine to grind a tree stump into small wood chips, removing it below the surface of the ground.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>How deep do you grind a stump?</h3>
<p>Most stumps are ground 4–12 inches below ground level, which is deep enough to plant grass or landscaping over the area.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>How long does stump grinding take?</h3>
<p>Most stumps take 15–60 minutes depending on size, species, and root system.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>How much does stump grinding cost?</h3>
<p>Costs depend on stump diameter, location, and accessibility. Most residential stumps range from $75–$400.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Do you remove the roots?</h3>
<p>Major surface roots can be ground down, but deep roots naturally decay underground over time.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Can grass grow after stump grinding?</h3>
<p>Yes. After grinding, we recommend removing excess wood chips and adding topsoil before planting grass.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Is stump grinding safe for my yard?</h3>
<p>Yes. Professional stump grinders are designed to minimize yard disturbance and protect surrounding landscaping.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Do I need to be home during service?</h3>
<p>Not always. As long as we have access to the stump and clear instructions, we can often complete the work while you're away.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Will stump grinding damage nearby structures?</h3>
<p>No. Our equipment is carefully controlled to avoid damage to fences, sidewalks, and buildings.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>What happens to the wood chips?</h3>
<p>Wood chips can be left for mulch, removed upon request, or spread around landscaping areas.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Can you grind stumps near fences or houses?</h3>
<p>Yes. We use smaller grinders for tight spaces to reach stumps near structures.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Why should I remove a tree stump?</h3>
<p>Stumps can attract pests, create tripping hazards, interfere with landscaping, and reduce property value.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Do stumps attract termites or pests?</h3>
<p>Yes. Rotting stumps can attract termites, ants, beetles, and other wood-boring insects.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>Can you grind multiple stumps?</h3>
<p>Yes. We offer discounted rates for properties with multiple stumps.</p>
</div>

<div class="faq-item">
<h3>How soon can you schedule service?</h3>
<p>Most stump grinding jobs can be scheduled within a few days depending on demand.</p>
</div>
